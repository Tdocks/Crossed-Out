# Crossed Out — Privacy Policy

_Draft for review. Starting template, not legal advice — have counsel review and
confirm it matches your actual data practices before publishing. Last updated:
2026-07-18._

Crossed Out ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect,
why, and your choices. Because a faith app necessarily touches sensitive topics,
we hold this data to a high standard.

## 1. Information we collect
- **Account:** email address (email/password sign-up) or an Apple-provided
  identifier (Sign in with Apple; you may use Apple's Hide My Email).
- **Faith & wellbeing inputs you provide:** your selected focus areas (e.g.
  anxiety, grief, addiction, marriage), daily mood check-ins, verse feedback,
  reflections/notes, and independent-study devotionals. **This can reveal
  sensitive personal circumstances, and we treat it as sensitive data.**
- **Usage:** in-app activity needed to personalize content (verses shown,
  completions, streaks) and privacy-preserving product analytics and crash
  reports (no advertising identifiers).
- We do **not** collect precise location unless you use a location-based feature
  and grant permission, and we do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs.

## 2. How we use it
- To personalize Scripture, devotionals, and guidance to what you're going
  through (the core function of the App).
- To operate features you use (community, saved content, streaks).
- To keep the community safe (reviewing reports, enforcing our Terms).
- To improve the App via aggregate, privacy-preserving analytics.
We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do **not** use your content
to train third-party foundation models.

## 3. AI processing
When you use AI features (the Kyra guide, AI devotional suggestions, semantic
search), the text you submit and relevant context are sent to our AI provider
(currently OpenAI) solely to generate a response. We send the minimum needed and
do not include your identity beyond what the feature requires. Our provider
processes this under its API terms and does not use API data to train its models.

## 4. Storage and security
Data is stored with our backend provider (Supabase) with row-level security so
each user can access only their own content. We use encryption in transit and
access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data.

## 5. Sharing
We share data only with service providers who help us run the App (hosting, AI
processing, analytics/crash reporting) under contract, or when required by law,
or to protect safety. Content you choose to post to community or share via Bridge
is visible to its intended audience.

## 6. Your choices and rights
- **Access/deletion:** delete your account and associated content anytime in
  Settings → Delete Account. This is permanent.
- **Regional rights:** depending on your location (e.g. GDPR/CCPA), you may have
  rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to certain
  processing. Contact us to exercise these.
- **Notifications:** manage in device settings.

## 7. Children
The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
their data. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us for removal.

## 8. Changes
We will post updates here and communicate material changes in-App.

## 9. Contact
Privacy questions or requests: privacy@[crossedout-domain].com
