Crossed Out

Scripture for Real Life

God didn't cross you out. He crossed out the debt and called you in.

Crossed Out is a quiet companion for daily faith. It personalizes Scripture to what you're actually carrying, walks with you through short daily devotions, connects you to a community of people praying and growing together, and helps you find a church home nearby. Read in the translation that speaks to you — BSB, WEB, or KJV.
