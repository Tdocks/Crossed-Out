Privacy Policy

Crossed Out: Daily Devotions  ·  Effective July 14, 2026
Developer: Tyler Dockswell  ·  Contact: tdoxwell@icloud.com

This policy explains, in plain language, what information Crossed Out collects, how it's used, where it's stored, and how you can control or delete it. Crossed Out is a personal, independently developed app, and this policy is written to describe our practices honestly and completely.

Information We Collect

Account data. If you create an account, we collect the email address you provide. If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive the name Apple shares with us. If you don't create an account, the app uses an anonymous session so your data still stays yours without identifying you personally.

Profile data you provide. Things like your first name, the focus areas you select, and what you tell us you need right now — used only to personalize your experience in the app.

Spiritual activity data. Your use of the app itself: daily check-ins and moods, streaks and completions, highlights, notes, and bookmarks you save, prayer requests and community posts you choose to share, bridge shares, churches you save, and taps indicating giving intent (for example, tapping through to a donation link).

Device and infrastructure data. We don't run our own tracking or analytics SDKs. The only device-level data collected is what's inherently logged by Apple's platform and our hosting infrastructure (Supabase) to operate the service — things like IP address and request timestamps.

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored with Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL database provider, in a United States region. Data sent between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS. We use row-level security so that your private data — check-ins, notes, bookmarks, saved churches, and similar records — can only be accessed by you. Content you deliberately share to the community is visible to other users, as described below.

The Kyra AI Guide

When you message Kyra, our in-app guide, your message is sent through our server-side proxy to OpenAI to generate a response. We do not use your Kyra conversations for advertising, and we don't sell them. Please don't share details of a medical emergency or crisis with Kyra — it isn't equipped to help in an emergency. If you're in crisis or danger, call your local emergency services immediately.

No Ads, No Data Sales

Crossed Out does not run advertising and does not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third-party data brokers.

Community Content

Prayer requests and posts you choose to share to the community are visible to other users of the app. Please keep that in mind before sharing sensitive details. The app includes in-app tools to report and block content or users you're concerned about.

Donations and Giving

Crossed Out helps you discover organizations and churches to give to, and links out to their websites when you choose to give. We never process or store your payment information — any donation happens directly on the third-party organization's site, under its own privacy and payment terms.

Deleting Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app: go to Settings → Delete Account. This removes your account and everything tied to it from our systems. You can also request deletion by emailing tdoxwell@icloud.com.

Children's Privacy

Crossed Out is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we'll update the effective date above and post the revised version at this page. This is a policy we've prepared ourselves, in plain language, and we'll keep it accurate as the app evolves.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email tdoxwell@icloud.com and we'll get back to you.
